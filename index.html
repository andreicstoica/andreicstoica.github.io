About Me

Hi, my name is Andrei Stoica. I'm an atypical techno-optimist working to help people directly, understanding that technological production is part of a broader system. Having pursued an individualized major titled 'Human Computer Interaction and Ethical Innovation Systems,' I was able to mix courses in the domains of computer science, hardware, design, ecology, and political economy. I am most interested in the intersections of all of these fields. Below you will find reflections on my previous work experience and other projects, which are just as valuable.
