- Date

Oct. '20 - May. '23

The Albert Gallatin Founders Fund is a school sanctioned grant fund run by students every semester. Each check can max out to a few thousand dollars. The purpose of the fund isn't to create returns, but to help aid in students' creative endeavours. Each semester since joining, I have conducted due diligence on ~15 applicants, vote to fund ideas with up to $2,500, and have maintained a mentor/coaching relationship with award winners. I have also taught new students venture capital frameworks given my previous experience.
