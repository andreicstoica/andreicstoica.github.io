- Project Name

BlocPower

- Roles

Product Manager

Web Designer

Copywriter


- Date

Jun. '21 - Aug. '21

BlocPower helps underserved communities retrofit old heating and cooling systems and install new wi-fi and solar systems through unique financing models and by working with city governments.

My work mainly aligned with a company OKR of increasing 'intake survey' click-through rates. As a product intern, I was tasked with reworking the website. After installing Hotjar (analytics and heatmapping software) on the website, it was clear that there was a lot to work on. I proposed an entire site redesign (link to mockups) , which highlighted the need to segment information (landing pages) to different visitors (types of customers). After some hesitation from the CEO, I built three new pillar-pages part of my redesign in Webflow to prove my hypothesis. The pages performeed exceptionally well, and with these metrics, BlocPower's Head of Product and I proposed a new website redesign with a product specification document . This convinced the CEO to contact web-dev agencies to contract the production of a completely new site.

I also worked closely with the growth marketing team to create campaign specific landing pages to sync with BlocPower's new HubSpot subscription and nationwide ad campaign.
