Locl.io aims to create insights for brick-and-mortar business locations through their Google Maps data.

I operated in two main roles at local. As a programmer, I coded a bulk image uploader and compressor for Google Maps images in Python that also included a post scheduling component. My tool decreased customer's time uploading images and planning Google Maps posts by at least 25%.

Code side-projects included: data analysis using Google Maps API to determine if weather impacted volume of sales for local businesses, and changing the organization of the dashboard.

As a marketer, I created numerous graphics and miscellaneous marketing content using my Adobe suite skills. These graphics were included in trade show appearances and email newsletters. I also created a comprehensive onboarding document (15 pages long) that included dozens of animated GIFs. This used Google's Codelabs service.
