XXcelerate Fund is a non-profit, seed-stage fund that helps create robust financial communities in the PNW by investing in women-led companies. You can learn more about the program by clicking this link.

I had three main tasks:

1. Interview more than a dozen cohort CEO's to produce short blog post style write-ups for XXcelerate's website and newsletter.

2. Worked alongside a developer and management to make a roadmap and mockup for new company website.

3. Helped plan a cohort graduation and demo day where 500+ people attended. I also helped secure the participation of Senator Ron Wyden.
